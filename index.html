About Climate Exchange Africa

Climate Exchange Africa Ltd is a climate-tech venture dedicated to enabling smallholder farmers and SMEs across Africa to access carbon markets and climate finance. Through a hybrid model that combines digital tools for emissions tracking, monitoring, reporting and verification (MRV), with on-ground support, the platform empowers users to adopt sustainable practices, reduce their carbon footprint, and generate value from climate action. By aggregating underserved producers and connecting them to global sustainability markets, Climate Exchange Africa bridges a critical gap in access, data, and financing. The initiative works in collaboration with farmer cooperatives, SME associations, carbon market players, and climate finance partners to ensure scalability, credibility, and long-term impact. Our goal is to make climate action practical, inclusive, and economically rewarding while driving green growth across the continent.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers
and SMEs across Africa to actively participate in the global climate economy by providing accessible digital tools, on-ground support, and market linkages
that enable them to measure, reduce, and monetize their carbon impact. We are committed to bridging the gap between underserved communities and carbon markets by simplifying emissions
tracking, promoting climate-smart practices, and unlocking access to climate finance. Through strategic partnerships, innovation, and community engagement, we aim to make climate action inclusive, scalable, and economically rewarding while driving sustainable development and green growth across the continent.
